How Does It Work?

📸

Image Capture

Upload an image from your device, or let the ESP32-CAM automatically capture and send images to the server.

🤖

Deep Learning Analysis

An EfficientNetV2-B0 multi-task model analyzes visual patterns — texture, color, and surface artifacts — to identify formalin treatment and rot.

🔥

Explainability (Grad-CAM)

A heatmap highlights exactly which regions of the fruit contributed to the prediction, ensuring trustworthy, interpretable AI decisions.

⚡

Instant Results

Server-side ONNX Runtime inference ensures results in under 1 second. The ESP32-CAM only sends images; all AI runs on the server.

⚠️ Important: This system detects visual proxies of adulteration (surface texture, color changes). It does not perform chemical analysis. For regulatory or safety-critical decisions, laboratory testing is required. Fruits: Formalin detection. Vegetables: Acid-wash detection. Does not detect wax, calcium carbide, or artificial colors.
